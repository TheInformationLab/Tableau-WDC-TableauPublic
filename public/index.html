How to find your Tableau Public Username?

  1. Go to Tableau Public
  2. Search for your account
  3. Find your username in the url as per the image below
  4. Copy the username and paste in the input box on the home page of this WDC
  5. Optional: Provide a comma separated list of users to get profile data for more users (e.g. userName1,userName2)
